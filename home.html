PLACEMENT CELL

The American College has been committed to excellence since its inception especially in terms of moulding the future of its students. The Placement Cell of The American College is committed to providing placement support to students matching their interest areas and academic profile and based on the demand from the external milieu. The cell focuses on students especially the outgoing (final year students) from both under-graduate and post-graduate programmes of aided and self-financed programmes. The placement Cell conducts placement related training and awareness programmes in the campus and organizes campus interviews and placement drives for the benefit of both aided and self-financed stream students.
